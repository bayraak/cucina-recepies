- 4 teaspoons of sugar
- 3 teaspoons of salt
- 1.5 teaspoons vegeta
- 3 teaspoons origano
- 3 teaspoons of parsley (black plastic spoon)
- 1 teaspoon of black pepper
- 1 bunch of fresh basil
- 1 spoon of dried basil
